About Our Website

Our website was made to help all those students out there who are struggling to keep up with their revision, and also for students like us, whose notes seem to have fast-running legs and hide-and-seek champion brains for reasons we cannot understand. This website was our end-year coding project, which was given by EchoedLabs after a year of weekly coding classes. Our website provides notes for various subjects in the lower secondary level. Not to worry, we will gradually add more subjects and subsequently, the upper secondary level. We really hope our website helps you in your revision. Do share our website with others hehe! Thank you!
